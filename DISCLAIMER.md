# Disclaimer

This MCP server provides Dutch land and woodland management data for informational purposes only.

## Not Professional Advice

The data served by this tool does not constitute professional legal, planning, or land management advice. Regulations, thresholds, and grant rates are based on published Dutch government sources, but individual circumstances vary. Requirements differ by gemeente, provincie, and site-specific designations (Natura 2000, monumentale bomen, etc.). Always consult your gemeente, provincie, or RVO before taking action.

## Data Sources

All data is sourced from:

- **Wet natuurbescherming (Wnb)** -- Rijksoverheid
- **Gemeentelijke APV** -- Lokale Regelgeving
- **Natura 2000 beheerplannen** -- Ministerie van LNV / Provincies
- **RVO Pachtbeleid** -- Rijksdienst voor Ondernemend Nederland
- **Kadaster** -- Erfpacht registratie
- **Nationale Bossenstrategie** -- Staatsbosbeheer / Ministerie van LNV
- **AERIUS Calculator** -- RIVM
- **Stichting Wandelnet** -- Klompenpaden en LAW-routes

All data is used under Dutch Government Open Data licence.

## Currency

Land management regulations change. Subsidy rates, thresholds, and processes are updated by government departments and provinces. This tool shows the data available at the time of its last ingestion run. Check `check_data_freshness` for the age of the data.
